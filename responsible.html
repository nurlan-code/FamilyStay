Our Environmental Initiatives

We believe in creating a better world for future generations through responsible tourism practices and environmental stewardship.

Carbon Neutral Operations

We've achieved carbon neutrality through renewable energy, energy-efficient systems, and carbon offset programs. Our goal is to be completely carbon negative by 2030.

100% Renewable Energy
-25% Carbon Footprint

Zero Waste Program

We've implemented comprehensive recycling and composting programs, eliminated single-use plastics, and work with local partners to minimize waste throughout our operations.

95% Waste Diverted
0 Single-Use Plastics

Water Conservation

We use advanced water-saving technologies, greywater recycling systems, and educate guests about water conservation to protect this precious resource.

40% Water Savings
100% Greywater Recycled

Local Sourcing

We source 80% of our food and products locally, supporting regional farmers and artisans while reducing transportation emissions and promoting local culture.

80% Local Sourcing

Reforestation Program

We plant one tree for every guest stay and maintain our own organic gardens. Our reforestation efforts have planted over 10,000 trees in the local area.

10,000+ Trees Planted
1:1 Tree per Stay

Our resort is powered by solar panels and we're expanding our renewable energy capacity. We also provide electric vehicle charging stations for eco-conscious guests.
