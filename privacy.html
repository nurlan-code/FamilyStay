Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your family's personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Travel preferences and special requests
  • Family member information including children's ages and dietary requirements
  • Communication preferences and feedback

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website or use our services, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Website usage data (pages visited, time spent, click patterns)
  • Location data (general geographic area, not precise location)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Important: We never collect sensitive information about children without explicit parental consent, and we take extra precautions to protect children's privacy.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

We use your information to:

  • Process reservations and manage your stay
  • Provide personalized services and recommendations
  • Communicate about your booking and stay
  • Handle special requests and accommodations
  • Ensure safety and security for all guests

2.2 Marketing and Communications

With your consent, we may use your information to:

  • Send promotional offers and newsletters
  • Share information about new services and activities
  • Invite you to participate in surveys and feedback programs
  • Send birthday and anniversary greetings

2.3 Legal and Safety Purposes

We may use your information to:

  • Comply with legal obligations and regulations
  • Protect against fraud and security threats
  • Respond to legal requests and court orders
  • Maintain records for business purposes

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Payment processing and financial transactions
  • Email and communication services
  • Customer support and reservation management
  • Marketing and advertising services
  • Analytics and website performance monitoring

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of our guests
  • Investigate or prevent illegal activities

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction, with appropriate privacy protections maintained.

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure servers and databases with restricted access
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy
  • Incident response procedures for data breaches

4.2 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Improve our services and customer experience

Security Note: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but continuously work to protect your information.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications

5.2 Communication Preferences

You can manage your communication preferences by:

  • Using the unsubscribe link in our emails
  • Contacting us directly at privacy@familystay.com
  • Updating your preferences in your account settings
  • Calling our customer service team

5.3 Cookies and Tracking

You can control cookies and tracking technologies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience.

6. Children's Privacy

6.1 Special Protections

We take special care to protect children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Parental consent is required for any data collection from minors
  • We limit the collection of children's information to what is necessary for our services
  • Parents can review, modify, or delete their children's information

6.2 Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:

  • Review information collected about their children
  • Request deletion of their children's information
  • Refuse further collection or use of their children's information
  • Contact us with any concerns about children's privacy

7. International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during international transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by relevant authorities
  • Adequacy decisions by data protection authorities
  • Certification under recognized privacy frameworks
  • Binding corporate rules for intra-group transfers

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Providing notice through our services
  • Obtaining consent for significant changes when required by law

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@familystay.com
  • Phone: +994 12 345 67 89
  • Address: FamilyStay Privacy Office, Family Paradise Resort, Paradise City, PC 12345
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@familystay.com

Response Time: We will respond to your privacy inquiries within 30 days of receipt. For urgent matters, please call our customer service team.

10. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 1, 2025, and was last updated on January 1, 2025. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
