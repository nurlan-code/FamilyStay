Family-Friendly Amenities

Every room comes equipped with amenities designed specifically for families, ensuring comfort and safety for guests of all ages.

Child Safety Features

Child-proof outlets, safety gates, corner guards, and secure windows ensure your little ones stay safe throughout their stay.

High-Speed WiFi

Complimentary high-speed internet access throughout the property, perfect for keeping kids entertained and parents connected.

Climate Control

Individual air conditioning and heating controls in every room, ensuring the perfect temperature for your family's comfort.

Smart Entertainment

Smart TVs with streaming services, children's channels, and family-friendly content to keep everyone entertained.

Mini-fridge, microwave, and coffee maker in most rooms, perfect for preparing snacks and light meals for your family.

Baby Equipment

Cribs, high chairs, baby monitors, and other essential equipment available upon request for families with infants.
