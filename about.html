About FamilyStay

Discover our story, values, and the passionate team behind your perfect family vacation experience.

Our Story

FamilyStay was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create spaces where families can truly connect, relax, and create lasting memories together. Founded in 2010 by a group of parents who understood the unique challenges of traveling with children, we've grown into a trusted name in family hospitality.

What started as a small boutique hotel has evolved into a network of family-friendly properties, each designed with love, care, and an understanding of what families need most. We believe that every family deserves a vacation where parents can unwind while children explore, play, and grow in a safe, nurturing environment.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional family hospitality experiences that create lasting memories while ensuring the safety, comfort, and happiness of every family member.

Our Vision

To be the leading family-friendly hotel chain, recognized globally for our innovative approach to family hospitality and our commitment to creating magical moments.

Our Promise

Every family that stays with us will experience personalized service, exceptional amenities, and a commitment to making their vacation truly special.

Our Core Values

These fundamental principles guide everything we do and shape the experiences we create for families.

We prioritize the safety and security of every family member, implementing comprehensive safety measures and child-proofing throughout our properties.

Exceptional Service

Our team is dedicated to providing personalized, attentive service that anticipates and exceeds your family's needs and expectations.

Sustainability

We're committed to environmental responsibility, implementing eco-friendly practices that protect our planet for future generations.

Family-Centered

Every decision we make is guided by what's best for families, ensuring our hotels truly serve the needs of parents and children alike.
