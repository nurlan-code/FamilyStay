Terms & Conditions

Please read these terms and conditions carefully before making a reservation at FamilyStay Hotels & Resorts. By booking with us, you agree to these terms.

1. Reservation Terms

1.1 Booking Confirmation

All reservations are subject to availability and confirmation. A valid credit card is required to secure your booking. You will receive a confirmation email with your reservation details upon successful booking.

1.2 Payment Terms

Payment terms vary by room type and season:

  • Standard rooms: Full payment required at time of booking
  • Family suites: 50% deposit required, balance due 30 days before arrival
  • Peak season bookings: Full payment required 60 days before arrival
  • Presidential suites: 75% deposit required, balance due 45 days before arrival

Important: All prices are quoted in USD and include applicable taxes and service charges unless otherwise specified. Family packages may include additional benefits.

2. Cancellation Policy

2.1 Standard Cancellation

Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before your scheduled arrival date. Cancellations made within 48 hours of arrival will be subject to a one-night room charge.

2.2 Peak Season Cancellation

During peak season (December 15 - January 15, July 1 - August 31), cancellations must be made at least 7 days before arrival to avoid charges. Cancellations within 7 days will incur a 100% charge.

2.3 Family Package Cancellation

Family packages require 14 days notice for full refund. Cancellations within 14 days will incur charges based on services already arranged (activities, meals, etc.).

2.4 No-Show Policy

Guests who fail to arrive on their scheduled check-in date without prior notice will be charged the full amount of their reservation.

3. Check-in and Check-out

3.1 Check-in Times

Check-in time is 3:00 PM. Early check-in requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges. We will do our best to accommodate early arrivals when possible.

3.2 Check-out Times

Check-out time is 12:00 PM. Late check-out requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges. Late check-out after 6:00 PM will be charged at the full nightly rate.

3.3 Identification Requirements

All guests must present valid government-issued photo identification upon check-in. International guests must present a valid passport. Parents must provide identification for all children in their party.

4. Family Policies

4.1 Age Requirements

Guests must be at least 18 years old to make a reservation. Guests under 18 must be accompanied by an adult guardian. Children under 12 stay free when sharing a room with parents.

4.2 Child Safety

All rooms are equipped with child safety features. Parents are responsible for supervising their children at all times. We provide safety equipment upon request including cribs, high chairs, and outlet covers.

4.3 Smoking Policy

FamilyStay is a non-smoking property. Smoking is prohibited in all indoor areas, including guest rooms, restaurants, and public spaces. Designated smoking areas are available outdoors away from family areas.

4.4 Pet Policy

Pets are not allowed on the resort property, with the exception of certified service animals. Service animals must be registered with the front desk upon arrival and must be properly trained.

4.5 Dress Code

Resort casual attire is appropriate throughout the property. Formal dining venues may require smart casual or formal attire. Swimwear is restricted to pool and beach areas only.

5. Activities and Services

5.1 Activity Bookings

All activities and services are subject to availability and weather conditions. We recommend booking activities in advance to ensure availability. Some activities may have age restrictions.

5.2 Liability

Guests participate in activities at their own risk. FamilyStay is not liable for any injuries or damages that may occur during activities, except where negligence can be proven.

5.3 Equipment and Safety

All safety equipment must be worn during water sports and adventure activities. Guests who refuse to wear required safety equipment will not be permitted to participate.

5.4 Babysitting Services

Babysitting services are provided by certified caregivers. Advanced booking is required. Parents must provide emergency contact information and medical information for their children.

6. Dining and Special Requests

6.1 Dietary Requirements

We accommodate special dietary requirements including allergies, religious restrictions, and medical needs. Please inform us of any requirements at least 48 hours before arrival.

6.2 Children's Meals

Children's menus are available at all restaurants. Special meal times can be arranged for families with young children. High chairs and booster seats are available upon request.

6.3 Birthday Celebrations

We offer special birthday packages for children. Advanced booking is required. Custom decorations and special treats can be arranged for an additional fee.

7. Privacy and Data Protection

7.2 Children's Privacy

We take special care to protect children's privacy. We do not collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents can review and delete their children's information at any time.

7.3 Marketing Communications

With your consent, we may send you marketing communications about our services and special offers. You can opt out of these communications at any time.
